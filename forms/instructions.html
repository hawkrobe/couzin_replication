Instructions

At the end of these instructions, you will be required to answer a brief quiz to assess your understanding. This quiz should be straight-forward if you read the instructions. If you fail this quiz, however, you will not be able to play the game, so read these instructions carefully.

After finishing the instructions, you will then enter a waiting room for a game. Some games require multiple participants, so you may have to wait for other players to join before the game will start. The maximum time you will spend in the waiting room is five minutes. While you are waiting for other players, feel free to familiarize yourself with the controls in the game. The game controls are the same in the waiting room as in the real game.

Once the game starts, you will see a screen that looks much like the following:

You will be in control of the blue triangle. You will start out moving at a constant speed. Holding down space bar will increase your speed. The left and right arrow keys will control the direction of your movement. You will have a score at each point in the game, indicated by the percentage shown at the top of the page. Each location in the game area is associated with a certain score. Your goals is to locate yourself in high scoring locations for as long as possible. You will receive a cash bonus based on your cumulative score in the game. The scores of locations will change over time, so you will have to continuously be ready to move around to maximize your bonus. Note that in the waiting room, the score for every location will be zero, but this will change once the game starts.

You will also be able to see the other participants who are currently playing in your game. These players can also see you. The other players get the same scores as you do at the same locations, and there is no competition between players. After the game ends, you will receive a survey code to submit with your HIT. You must submit the code exactly as shown to receive your bonuses.
